Equipment

The main equipment in PIC is a Bruker Ultima in vivo multiphoton microscope equipped with an Insight X3 broadly tunable, ultrafast laser system. In addition to the multiphoton laser, a 473 nm and 561 nm continous wave laser lines and a high power 631 nm LED are also coupled to the microscope making possible to conduct simultaneous imaging and photostimulation experiments using wide range of inhibitory and excitatory photosensitive opsins.

High speed volumetric scannig is also possible up to 400 um wiht a Z-axis piezo drive
