Equipment

ProLab has several tools needed for rapid prototyping including: SLA and FDM 3D printers and a laser cutter. We can provide help for using the appropriate CAD software to make your own design and train you up to use the available equipmnent for building and finalizing your prototype in the workshop area. You can find a few examples of behavior equipment which were build in ProLAB in the Gallery section.
