Tools and methods

ProLab has several tools needed for rapid prototyping, including SLA and FDM 3D printers and a laser cutter. We can provide help with using the appropriate CAD software to make your own designs and train you to use the available equipment for building and finalizing your prototype in the workshop area. You can find a few examples of behavioral equipment built in ProLab in the Gallery section.
