Zvi S Goldstein

Zvi Goldstein graduated from Yeshiva University with a BA in Physics in May 2020, and is currently pursuing a Masters in Electrical Engineering at Columbia University, with a focus in Signal Processing and Optics. Zvi joined the lab in Fall 2020, and is contributing to our work in determining if mossy cells are responsible for spatial memory amongst mice.
