Ruojun Li

Ruojun Li is pursuing a master's degree in Statistics at Columbia University. She obtained her bachelor's degree in Statistics from Wuhan University. She is now interested in applying statistics theory in the real world and gaining data analysis experience. She joined the lab in the Spring of 2024 and started working with Gergely Turi on predicting the long-term behavior effects of stress-enhanced fear learning(SEFL) in mice.
