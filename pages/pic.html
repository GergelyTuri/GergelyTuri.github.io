Tools and methods

The main equipment in PIC is a Bruker Ultima in vivo multiphoton microscope equipped with an Insight X3 broadly tunable, ultrafast laser system. In addition to the multiphoton laser, two continuous-wave laser lines (473 and 561 nm) and a high-power LED (631 nm) are also coupled to the microscope, making it possible to conduct simultaneous imaging and photostimulation experiments using a wide range of inhibitory and excitatory photosensitive opsins.
