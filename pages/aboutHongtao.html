Hongtao Cai

Hongtao Cai graduated from Columbia University with a Master's Degree in Electrical Engineering in December 2021, with a focus in VLSI. He received his Bachelor's degree from University of Colorado Boulder. Hongtao joined the lab in Fall 2020 and contributed to our work related to our Head fixed Mouse VR system. Hongtao is currently working at Allegro Microsystems as an Electrical Engineer.

Publictions from the lab:

Hongtao was part of the team which developed our simple-to-use virtual reality system for mice. The publication is now accepted in a in JoVE and will be published soon.
